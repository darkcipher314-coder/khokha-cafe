Rooted in Comfort.

Khokha is more than a cafe; it is a sanctuary. Inspired by the earthy tones of nature and the warmth of community, we craft an atmosphere where time slows down.

Whether you are here for our single-origin pour-overs in the morning or our curated cocktails under the dim lights of the lounge, Khokha is your home away from home.
